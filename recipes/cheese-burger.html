Cheese Burger

Description

Easy cheese burger recipe that taste delicious.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beef Burger
  • Mayo
  • Tomatoe
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese Slice
  • Pickle

Steps

  1. Cook the beef burger in the frying pan
  2. Assemble bun and add beef patty
  3. Top with lettuce, tomatoe, pickle and mayo
  4. Add your cheese slice
