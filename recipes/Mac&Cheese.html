Mac & Cheese

Description

Welcome to this delicious Mac and Cheese recipe. This is an easy to follow recipe with little ingredients and no compromise on flavour.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in boiling water
  2. Mix milk and flour together with butter
  3. Melt cheese into this
  4. Mix pasta in
  5. Pour into Pyrex dish and add breadcrumbs on top
  6. Cook in oven for 10 minutes/ Gas Mark 5
